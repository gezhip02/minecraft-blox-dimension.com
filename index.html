How to Play

Get started with these simple steps to begin your Minecraft Block Explorer adventure

1

Choose Your Game Mode

Select either Survival mode for a challenging adventure where you'll need to gather resources and defend yourself, or Creative mode for unlimited blocks and flying abilities to build freely.

2

Learn the Controls

Move your character with the WASD keys. Sprint by double-tapping A or D. Collect resources by clicking and holding on nearby blocks or creatures. Place blocks by right-clicking on an empty space.

3

Craft and Build

Open your inventory by pressing E. Here, you can craft items and equip armor. Combine materials to create tools, weapons, and building blocks to construct your shelter.

4

Survive and Explore

In Survival mode, you'll need to manage your hunger, health, and defend against hostile mobs. Build a shelter before nightfall when monsters come out. Explore caves, find rare resources, and expand your world.

5

Join Multiplayer (Optional)

For an extra challenge, join the online scavenger hunt where you can team up with friends to find all 10 items in just 8 minutes before anyone else!

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Minecraft Block Explorer have multiplayer?

Yes, the game offers a multiplayer mode. You can team up with friends online to compete in a scavenger hunt, racing to find all 10 items in just 8 minutes before anyone else!

When was Minecraft Block Explorer released?

Minecraft Block Explorer was originally released in Flash format and has been rebuilt in HTML5. The HTML5 version has been available since 2021 and receives regular updates from the development team.

What's the difference between Survival and Creative mode?

In Survival mode, you need to collect resources, craft tools, build shelter, and defend against hostile mobs. You have limited health and hunger to manage. In Creative mode, you have unlimited resources, can fly, and won't take damage, allowing you to focus entirely on building and creating.

Can I save my progress in the game?

Yes, your worlds and creations are saved automatically. This allows you to continue your adventure whenever you return to the game. You can also manually save your progress through the in-game menu.

What are the system requirements?

Minecraft Block Explorer is a browser-based game that runs on HTML5, so it requires a relatively modern browser. It works on most devices including desktops, laptops, tablets, and even some mobile phones. No installation or plugins are required.
