How to Play

  1. Choose Your Mode: Select from various game modes including BloxdHop (parkour), DoodleCube (creative drawing), Peaceful (sandbox building), and CubeWarfare (combat).
  2. Master the Controls: Use WASD to move, Shift or double-tap W to run, Space to jump, and C or Z to crouch. Press T or Enter to chat with other players.
  3. Complete Challenges: In BloxdHop mode, navigate through challenging obstacle courses by timing your jumps accurately to reach the end of the map.
  4. Build and Create: In Peaceful mode, collect resources and build your own structures in a sandbox environment similar to Minecraft's creative mode.
  5. Battle Others: In combat modes like CubeWarfare, compete against other players in exciting battles while also building defensive structures.

Pro Tip: Use commands like "/rtv" to vote for skipping the current map, "/players" to see who's in your lobby, and "/xp" to check your level and experience points.

When was Minecraft Blox Dimension released? +

Minecraft Blox Dimension was officially released in early 2021 and has been continuously updated with new features and game modes since then.

Yes, Minecraft Blox Dimension is safe to play. As of 2024, there have been no reports of private information leaks. We recommend creating a personal account to ensure safe playing and to save your progress.

What are the different game modes available? +

Minecraft Blox Dimension offers a variety of game modes including BloxdHop (parkour challenges), DoodleCube (creative drawing), EvilTower (vertical parkour), Peaceful (sandbox building), CubeWarfare (combat), Survival, Creative, Bedwars, Sky Wars, and many more!

Does the game work on mobile devices? +
